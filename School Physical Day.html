Who is Eligible Navarre High School Student-Athletes

Entering grades 9-12 who are participating in FHSAA sports ONLY.

What is Included Comprehensive Screening

Height, weight, vision, orthopaedic movement screening, blood pressure, and physician/provider evaluation.

Critical Guidelines

  • Required Form: The 4-page FHSAA EL2 Physical Form (version "Revised 2/26" or later) is the ONLY accepted form. Print it single-sided; do not fold pages.
  • Signatures Required: Parent/guardian signatures are required on pages 2 & 4. Fill out all history/demographic info prior to arrival.
  • Apparel: Wear athletic attire. Bring and wear any prescription glasses or contact lenses.
  • Next Steps: Once physical forms are complete, they are returned to the student. Families are responsible for submitting page 4 (Medical Eligibility) to the school.

ECG Screening Information

New Florida Law Requirement: ECG screenings are now mandatory for new FHSAA athletic participants starting this year.

Who Needs an ECG Screening?

Mandatory

Rising 2026 - 2027 Freshmen

All incoming freshmen trying out for or participating in FHSAA interscholastic sports.

Mandatory

New Athletes (Grades 10-12)

Students in grades 10-12 who did NOT participate in FHSAA athletics previously.

Exemptions & Exclusions: Returning FHSAA student-athletes are NOT required to have an ECG, though they may receive one if desired. ECG screenings are NOT required for Band, ROTC, Dance, or Flagline.
